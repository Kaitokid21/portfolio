Alloom Platform: System Design Project

This project was carried out during the course System Design. The project focuses on the lack of organization in the Rural Marketplace with the context of handloom production and sells in northeastern states. The project concluded with a system and a platform. The system includes logistics, stakeholders and the relations among them, etc. Aloom, the platform, consists of a kiosk which helps the handloom producers get orders and pitch their products and an app which assist in pitching the products online.

  • Project Mentor: Prof. Praful Kalita
  • Duration: January, 2016 - April, 2016
  • App: System Design, UI for Alloom app and Kiosk
  • Softwares: Adobe Illustrator, Adobe Photoshop, Xmind
